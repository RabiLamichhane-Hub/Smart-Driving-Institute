How It Works

Scheduling rules

  • The scheduler runs every night and assigns your session for the next day.
  • It tries your 1st choice slot first.
  • If that slot is full, it checks the nearest available slot outward.
  • A slot is not guaranteed — it depends on resource availability.
  • Update your preferences any time before 10 PM to affect tomorrow's schedule.
